## Objectives

This dashboard allows to explore the outputs of occupancy models on odonates distribution in Europe produced by the DRAGON project.   

This is a beta version with preliminary modelling results, but it will be updated with final results. 

## About DRAGON

The [DRAGON research project](https://www.fondationbiodiversite.fr/en/the-frb-in-action/programs-and-projects/le-cesab/dragon/) focuses on odonates (dragonflies and damselflies), a charismatic and important but often neglected taxonomic group that provides a unique opportunity to **study anthropogenic impacts on interface ecosystems linking terrestrial and freshwater environments**.

The DRAGON research project was selected from the [2022 FRB-MTE-OFB "Antropogenic pressures and impacts on terrestrial biodiversity](https://www.fondationbiodiversite.fr/en/calls/appel-a-projets-frb-mte-ofb-2022-impacts-sur-la-biodiversite-terrestre-dans-lanthropocene/).
